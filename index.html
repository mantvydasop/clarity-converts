The C.L.E.A.R. framework for homepages that turn attention into action.

This is the structure I use to turn complex products into clear, conversion focused homepages.

C
Context

Anchor buyers in the right problem and situation before you talk about features. When context is wrong, everything else feels off, no matter how good the product is.

L
Language

Use the words your buyers already use to describe their pains, goals and constraints. Precise language cuts through noise and earns attention fast.

E
Evidence

Back up your claims with proof, numbers and real world examples instead of generic statements. Evidence turns a nice story into a believable one.

A
Architecture

Structure the page so every section has a job, leads to the next and pushes buyers closer to a decision. No orphan sections, no copy that just ‚Äúsits‚Äù there.

R
Rhythm

Control pacing through contrast, short and long sections, and strategic repetition. Rhythm keeps buyers reading instead of bouncing.
